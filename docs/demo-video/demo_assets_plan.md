# Demo Assets Plan

## Suggested Screenshots

Capture these stills for thumbnails, landing pages, and social posts:

1. LinkedIn profile with extension sidebar open.
2. PROFILE card showing name, headline, company, location, and profile URL.
3. Lead Score and Persona section.
4. Suggested DM card.
5. Bottom action buttons with success message.
6. HubSpot test contact with LinkedIn URL saved.
7. HubSpot note attached to the contact.
8. HubSpot follow-up task or fallback follow-up note.
9. End card with product name and CTA.

## Suggested Screen Recording Sequence

1. Start on a visible LinkedIn profile.
2. Open the extension sidebar.
3. Show Beta Pro active badge if recording the full paid workflow.
4. Click "Analyze Profile".
5. Pause for 2 seconds on the completed analysis.
6. Click "First DM".
7. Pause on the Suggested DM.
8. Click "Copy DM".
9. Click "Add to HubSpot".
10. Pause on the success message.
11. Click "Create HubSpot Note".
12. Click "Create Follow-up Task".
13. Optional: switch to HubSpot test contact and show the saved contact timeline.
14. End with product name and CTA.

## Aspect Ratios

16:9:
- Best for Product Hunt, landing page hero video, YouTube, Loom, and sales pages.
- Recommended resolution: 1920x1080.

1:1:
- Good for LinkedIn feed and X/Twitter feed.
- Recommended resolution: 1080x1080.

4:5:
- Strong for LinkedIn mobile feed.
- Recommended resolution: 1080x1350.

9:16:
- Optional for vertical X/Twitter or short-form reuse.
- Recommended resolution: 1080x1920.
- Crop around the extension sidebar and the main LinkedIn profile area.

## Export Settings

Video:
- Format: MP4
- Codec: H.264
- Frame rate: 30 fps
- Resolution: 1920x1080 for master export
- Bitrate: 8 to 12 Mbps

Audio:
- Format: AAC
- Sample rate: 48 kHz
- Bitrate: 160 to 192 kbps

Subtitles:
- Burn in subtitles for social clips.
- Keep subtitle lines under 48 characters when possible.
- Use high contrast white text with dark shadow or semi-transparent background.

## Visual Style

- Clean B2B SaaS look.
- Use soft gray background when adding title cards.
- Use teal or dark slate accents to match the extension UI.
- Avoid noisy transitions.
- Use simple zooms only when text is small.

## Privacy And Safety Edits

- Blur personal emails.
- Blur phone numbers.
- Blur private notes.
- Blur HubSpot owner names if needed.
- Do not show API URLs with admin paths.
- Do not show license keys or tokens.
- Do not imply automatic LinkedIn messaging.

## Recommended Thumbnail Text

- "LinkedIn to HubSpot in one sidebar"
- "Analyze, draft, sync"
- "Less CRM copy-paste"
- "AI assistant for HubSpot prospecting"
