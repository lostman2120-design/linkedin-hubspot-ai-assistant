# LinkedIn to HubSpot AI Assistant Demo Script

Length: 45 to 75 seconds  
Format: screen recording with voiceover and light captions  
Audience: B2B founders, SDRs, BDRs, RevOps teams, HubSpot users

## 0:00-0:05 - Hook

What to show on screen:
- A LinkedIn profile page with the extension sidebar open.
- Keep the profile public-facing and avoid showing private inboxes, emails, or customer records.

Narration:
"If your team prospects on LinkedIn and updates HubSpot by hand, this is the workflow this extension replaces."

Subtitle:
"Stop copying LinkedIn profile details into HubSpot by hand."

On-screen caption:
"LinkedIn to HubSpot, without the copy-paste."

## 0:05-0:14 - Analyze The Visible Profile

What to show on screen:
- Click "Analyze Profile".
- Show the profile card filling in with name, headline, company, location, and profile URL.

Narration:
"Open one visible LinkedIn profile, then click Analyze Profile. The extension reads only the information already visible on this current page."

Subtitle:
"Click Analyze Profile on the current visible profile."

On-screen caption:
"Analyze a visible LinkedIn profile"

## 0:14-0:25 - AI Lead Score And Summary

What to show on screen:
- Show Lead Score, Persona, Pain Points, Icebreaker, and Next Action.
- Keep the sidebar centered and easy to read.

Narration:
"It creates an AI lead score, identifies the likely persona, highlights possible pain points, and gives your rep a clear next action."

Subtitle:
"Get lead score, persona, pain points, and next action."

On-screen caption:
"Generate an AI lead score"

## 0:25-0:37 - Draft Outreach

What to show on screen:
- Click "First DM" or "Connection Message".
- Show the Suggested DM card.
- Click "Copy DM".

Narration:
"Then it drafts a concise, natural LinkedIn message your rep can review, edit, and copy. It never sends messages automatically."

Subtitle:
"Draft a personalized LinkedIn message. Review before sending."

On-screen caption:
"Draft a personalized DM"

## 0:37-0:52 - Save To HubSpot

What to show on screen:
- Click "Add to HubSpot".
- Show success status near the bottom buttons.
- Optionally switch to a HubSpot test contact page if it is safe and sanitized.

Narration:
"When the lead looks useful, save or update the person in HubSpot with the real name, company, title, and LinkedIn URL."

Subtitle:
"Save the contact to HubSpot with one click."

On-screen caption:
"Save to HubSpot"

## 0:52-1:05 - Create Note And Follow-up Task

What to show on screen:
- Click "Create HubSpot Note".
- Click "Create Follow-up Task".
- Show success messages.

Narration:
"It can also create an AI summary note and a follow-up task, so your CRM stays clean and your next step is not forgotten."

Subtitle:
"Create notes and follow-up tasks in HubSpot."

On-screen caption:
"Create notes and follow-up tasks"

## 1:05-1:15 - Safety And CTA

What to show on screen:
- Return to extension sidebar.
- Show Free plan or Beta Pro badge.
- End with product name and URL or Chrome Web Store page.

Narration:
"This is not a scraper, crawler, or auto-messaging tool. It helps sales teams move faster while the user stays in control. Try LinkedIn to HubSpot AI Assistant today."

Subtitle:
"No auto-messaging. No crawling. The user stays in control."

On-screen caption:
"No auto-messaging. No crawling."

CTA:
"Install the Chrome extension and try it on your next LinkedIn prospect."
